About Me

I am an aspiring data analyst with a strong passion for turning data into meaningful insights. I have a background in mathematics and statistics, and I love working with numbers and analyzing patterns.

My journey into data analysis began during my working years, where I took courses in data analysis, database management, and programming. I quickly realized the power of data in driving informed decision-making and solving complex problems.

I am proficient in Excel, SQL, Python, and data visualization tools like Tableau and PowerBI. I enjoy cleaning and transforming data, conducting exploratory data analysis, and building predictive models. I am also familiar with statistical concepts and techniques that help uncover valuable insights.

My goal as a data analyst is to leverage my skills to extract meaningful insights from data and support data-driven decision-making. I am excited about the potential of data to drive innovation and solve real-world problems.

Outside of data analysis, I enjoy staying updated with the latest advancements in the field through online courses and participating in data analysis competitions. I am always eager to learn new techniques and tools that can enhance my analytical abilities.
